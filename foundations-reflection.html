MY FOUNDATIONS JOURNEY

What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

Before dev academy I was a nervous and shy person to reach out anyone and hesitation on my communication skills. I was thinking wrong as it may give wrong impression going to other for help however after finishing couple sprints, I was socialising myself into foundation channel and seeking help on my errors also helping other ppl to solve their piece of codes.

By writing reflections on each sprint, I improved my confidence to next level. I realized the importance of Conscious Listening and also, I learnt how to emotionally connect with other people. I learnt organizing and preplanning the technical challenges that also helped me to manage my time efficiently
